How To Play

  • Your aim is to correctly guess the word or phrase from a set of emojis.
  • It could be a category, film, book, character, etc.
  • There are 20 new sets of emojis every day for you to guess. You will be tested on 5 at random.
  • You'll be timed to see how long it takes to guess all 5 sets
  • Race with your friends to see who can deduce what the emojis are communicating first
  • Hit SUBMIT to see if your answer is correct and to move on to the next set of emojis
  • If given the message “Almost, you are close!” it means your guess is part of the answer (e.g. photo and photos, hat and top hat)
  • Come back tomorrow for a fresh set of emojis and a new challenge!
