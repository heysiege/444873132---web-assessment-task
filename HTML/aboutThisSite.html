About This Site

WHAT IS OVERTHINKER?

Overthinker is a puzzle-based website, containing a variety of brain twisters and puzzles to challenge your thinking skills and problem solving. This includes a daily game where the user must decipher the meaning of a series of emojis, a collection of riddles, trivia questions, and logic puzzles on different topics, a library of downloadable and printable visual puzzles, a blog with a variety of posts on interesting topics and an email newsletter signup. The goal of the website is to engage users in a fun and interactive way, and be a fun destination for all-things puzzles and brain twisters.
