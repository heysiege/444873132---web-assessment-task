Food & Drink Riddles

What can provide food before it is alive, while it is alive and after it's dead?
Answer: Chicken
I'm a sad fruit that can't be cheered up. What am I?
Answer: A blueberry
I'm a food that's always eaten by the wrong person. What am I?
Answer: Nachos
I'm a seed with three letters in my name. If you take away the last two, I'd stay the same. What am I?
Answer: A pea

Animals and Nature Riddles

First, you see me in the grass dressed in yellow; next I am in dainty white, then I fly away. What am I?
Answer: A dandelion
The pearls of night, stolen in clearest light of day. What am I?
Answer: Dew
I can be very strong, but other times I'm light. You can't see me move but you can see my effects. I'm measured on a special type of scale but I don't weigh anything. What am I?
Answer: The wind
I fly without wings, I cry without eyes. What am I?
Answer: A cloud

Character Riddles

Lifeless eyes on my smiling face and watch your child's sleeping place. In their dreams they hold me tight.
Answer: A teddy bear
A father's child, a mother's child, yet no one's son. Who am I?
Answer: The daughter
I have a heart that never beats, I have a home but I never sleep. I can take a man's house and build anothers, And I love to play games with my many brothers. I am a king among fools. Who am I?
Answer: The king of hearts in a deck of cards.
I am wrapped but I am not a gift, I am kept neatly in a chamber and Archeologists find me as a great treasure. What am I?
Answer: A mummy

Answer: A vase
Can you find something which has keys that open no locks, with space but no room and allows you to enter but not go in?
Answer: A keyboard
I sit in a corner while travelling around the world. What am I?
Answer: A stamp
I am tall when young and short when I am old, what am I?
Answer: A candle
